202's Best Summer Cook

With the best of the season coming from "orchads, farms and gardens," NPR has put together an impressive collection of 10 summer cookbooks.

Summer is, after all, so often a time of accidental vegetarianism. It's when even carnivorous folk(myself among them) are seduced by fields and farm stands, lured in by lettuce and tempted by tomatoes. We don't necessarily set out to go meatless. But a couple of fruit smoothies and several bowl's of baby greens later in the lazy heat of a stove-avoiding afternoon, that0s just what happens. And so, to help you to make the most of summer's bountry, here are 10 cookbooks that take their inspiration from that most peaceful of kindoms - the plant kindom.
